Ways to mitigate Poverty

Yes we can, together!

Know how

Increase access to Education

Education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Providing quality education and vocational training can equip individuals with the skills needed to secure better-paying jobs and improve their economic prospects.

Improve access to healthcare

Poor health can be a major contributor to poverty. By improving access to healthcare services, especially in rural areas, people can receive medical treatment and avoid costly healthcare bills that can push them further into poverty.

Implement social welfare programs

The government can introduce social welfare programs, such as food subsidies, pensions, and unemployment benefits, to provide a safety net for the poor and help them cope with financial shocks.
